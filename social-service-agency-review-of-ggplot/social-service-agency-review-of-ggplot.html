Basic ggplot

A ggplot call consists of the data, an aesthetic, geometrie(s), and options.

Histogram

hide 
ggplot(data = clients, # data I am using
       aes(x = age)) + # what I am graphing
  geom_histogram() # how I am graphing it

Density

hide 
ggplot(data = clients, # data I am using
       aes(x = age)) + # what I am graphing
  geom_density(fill = "blue") # how I am graphing it

Bar Chart of Means

Bar chart of means of a continuous variable by a categorical variable.

hide 
ggplot(data = clients, # data I am using
       aes(x = program, # what I am graphing
           y = mental_health_T2)) + # what I am graphing
  stat_summary(fun = mean, # summarizing y 
               geom = "bar", # with bars
               fill = "royalblue")

Get Some Extra Information on the Graph!

Add information to the aesthetic.

Color as information.

hide 
ggplot(data = clients, # data I am using
       aes(x = age, # what I am graphing
           y = mental_health_T2, # what I am graphing
           color = gender)) + # adding info to aesthetic
  geom_point() + # how I am graphing it 
  geom_smooth() # how I am graphing it

Try adding color as information with one of the bar charts from earlier.

Add Labels and Options

https://jrnold.github.io/ggthemes/

hide 
library(ggthemes) # for themes

ggplot(data = clients, # data I am using
       aes(x = age, # what I am graphing
           y = mental_health_T2, # what I am graphing
           color = gender)) + # what I am graphing
  geom_point() + # how I am graphing it
  geom_smooth() + # how I am graphing it
  facet_wrap(~gender) + # facet wrap on gender
  labs(title = "Mental Health By Age",
       subtitle = "by Gender Identity",
       x = "age",
       y = "mental health") +
  scale_color_viridis_d() + # beautiful colors!
  theme_minimal()
