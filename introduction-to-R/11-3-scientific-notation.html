11.3 Scientific Notation

R will, by default, often make use of scientific notation to express very large, or very small numbers, e.g.Â \(1.03 \times 10^7\) instead of \(1,030,000\), or \(1.03 \times 10^{-7}\) to express \(.000000103\).

Sometimes you will want to turn off this use of scientific notation.

# heavily penalize the use of scientific notation
# i.e. turn off scientific notation

options(scipen=999)
