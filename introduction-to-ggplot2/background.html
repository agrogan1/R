1 Background

R has a number of graphing libraries, including base graphics that are installed whenever you install R.

ggplot2, is a graphing library in R that makes beautiful graphs. ggplot2 graph syntax can be formidably complex, with a somewhat steep learning curve.

That being said, learning ggplot2 is worth the effort for a couple of reasons. First, the graphs are beautiful. Second, ggplot2âs syntax, though seemingly arcane at times, forces you to think about the nature of your data, and the ideas that you are graphing. Lastly, a little bit of knowledge about ggplot2 can go a long way, and can build a powerful foundation for future learning.
